Before running the demo, go to the 'image' routine and click on 'code'.
Under 'Begin Experiment', change eye_tracker_name to the eye tracker you used. 

Eye tracker supported:
'REDm'
'HiSpeed'
'RED'
'REDn_Professional'
'REDn_Scientific'
'RED250mobile'

'stop.jpg' is sent to as a message at trial offset, and is included to be able to import an visualize the results into BeGaze.
